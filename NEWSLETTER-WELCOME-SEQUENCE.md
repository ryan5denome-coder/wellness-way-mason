**Preview text:**

**Body:**

Quick follow-up.

When patients come in tired, foggy, bloated, anxious, or just generally *off* — and their labs come back "normal" — there's almost always something underneath that nobody's measured.

We use a framework called **the 3 T's**: Traumas, Toxins, and Thoughts. Three categories of stress that drive inflammation, and inflammation drives most of the chronic patterns we see.

**Traumas** — physical stress your body has absorbed. Old injuries, repetitive strain, the way you sit at your desk, structural compensations from years of holding tension. The body remembers. Chiropractic adjustments and movement assessment reveal what's still held in your structure.

**Toxins** — chemical stress that accumulates quietly. Food sensitivities, environmental exposures, medication side effects, water and air quality. They build up. Comprehensive lab work shows where your body is fighting battles you didn't know you had.

**Thoughts** — emotional stress is physiological. It shows up as nervous-system dysregulation, hormone shifts, sleep disturbance, gut symptoms. You can't out-supplement a chronically activated stress response.

You may have one of these driving the bus. You may have all three. **The point isn't to guess — it's to test.**

**Body:**

Last one in this welcome series — and probably the most practical.

If you've ever thought about coming in but weren't sure what a first visit even looks like, here's the honest walkthrough.

**1. The Discovery Consultation (60–75 minutes)**

We listen first. Bring your story, your history, any labs you already have, and the questions you've been holding. We're not interested in a 7-minute "what are your symptoms" interview. We want to understand the timeline of how you got here.

**2. Comprehensive Testing — Recommended, Never Required**

Based on what we hear, we'll recommend a testing panel. You see the cost, the rationale, and what each test reveals *before* anything is ordered. You stay in control. Some patients want the full picture from day one. Others want to start small and add later. Both are fine.

Common panels we use:
- Complete thyroid panel (TSH, free T3, free T4, reverse T3, antibodies)
- DUTCH hormone panel
- Cardiometabolic panel
- Comprehensive food allergy testing
- Gut-health stool analysis (when relevant)

**3. Review of Findings + Health Restoration Plan**

Once results are in, we walk through them with you. Not in 5 minutes — in detail. You leave with a plan that includes:
- Chiropractic care (when structural patterns apply)
- Targeted nutrition and lifestyle changes
- Supplementation when nutrient gaps are real
- Coordination with your existing medical team

**4. We Work *Alongside* Your Doctors, Not in Place of Them**

This is important. We don't change medications. We don't replace psychiatric care. We don't tell you to ditch your cardiologist. Our role is to help you understand what's underneath the surface so you and your medical team can make better-informed decisions over time.
