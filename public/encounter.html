Body & spirit

Caring for your body is part of the calling.

"Do you not know that your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit within you?" — 1 Corinthians 6:19

Stewarding your health isn't vanity. It's so you can love your family, serve your church, and walk out your calling with energy that lasts. But when your body is sounding an alarm — pain, fatigue, a gut that won't settle — silencing the symptom isn't the same as finding the fire underneath it.

So we ask a different question: why is your body sounding the alarm? Then we test instead of guess, and we build a plan that fits your real life — always alongside your primary care doctor and specialists, never in place of them.

How we help

Two doors, the same approach.

Come in for the pain you can point to, or for the deeper testing-based work. Either way, we start by listening.

Chiropractic

Hands-on, specific adjustments.

For the conditions chiropractors are known for — back and neck pain, sciatica, headaches, sports injury and recovery, prenatal (Webster Technique), and gentle pediatric care.

Health Restoration

We don't guess. We test.

Functional lab work paired with nutrition guidance, a supplement care plan, and lifestyle support — for when symptoms keep coming back and you're ready to ask why.

  • We look at three kinds of stress underneath your symptoms: Traumas (physical), Toxins (chemical), and Thoughts (emotional).
  • We use real labs — Access, DUTCH, Genova, ImmunoLabs, and Mosaic — not guesswork.
  • We sit down and walk through your results in plain English, then build the plan with you.
