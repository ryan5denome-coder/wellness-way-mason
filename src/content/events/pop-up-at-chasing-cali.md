## When & Where

**Saturday, May 30, 2026 · 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM**

**Chasing Cali Collective**
418 W Benson St, Reading, OH 45215
([@chasingcali on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/chasingcali/) · [chasingcali.com](https://www.chasingcali.com))

## What's Happening

Dr. Ryan DeNome, DC will be on-site at Chasing Cali Collective offering chiropractic adjustments during their Saturday morning. Walk in, get adjusted, grab coffee, browse the spa menu, hang out with the Cali community — all in one stop.

This is a relaxed, no-pressure setup. Come for an adjustment, ask questions about lab testing or how we work, or just meet the team and see if The Wellness Way approach is a fit for you. No commitment beyond the visit.

## Pricing

- **First-time patients:** $65
- **Existing patients:** $45

Cash, card, HSA/FSA accepted.

## What to Expect

- A brief check-in to understand what's going on for you
- A targeted chiropractic adjustment from Dr. DeNome
- A few minutes to ask anything you've been curious about — labs, the 3 T's framework, whether what we do could help with the specific issue you're dealing with

Each visit runs about 15–20 minutes. Wear comfortable clothes you can move in.

## Walk-Ins Welcome

No appointment needed — just show up between 8 AM and 1 PM. If you'd prefer to lock in a specific time slot or have questions before you come, **call the clinic at (283) 223-8376** or DM us on Instagram [@thewellnessway.mason](https://www.instagram.com/thewellnessway.mason/).

Chasing Cali is a women-owned wellness collective in Reading, OH — spa services, coffee bar, beauty studios all under one roof. A community-first space built for the kind of slow-down Saturday morning that we're really into. Worth checking out even if chiropractic isn't your reason for visiting.
