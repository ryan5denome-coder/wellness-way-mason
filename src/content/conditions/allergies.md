If your nose runs every spring, your stomach feels like a balloon after meals, or your skin breaks out and you can't pin down why. Your immune system is trying to tell you something.

Allergies are one of the most over-explained and under-tested patterns we see at The Wellness Way - Mason. People assume "it's just pollen," "it's just dairy," or "it's just stress." Sometimes that's true. Often the real trigger list is longer, quieter, and connected to a gut and immune system that have been on alert for years.

You deserve to know what your body is actually reacting to.

## Symptoms Patients Describe to Us

If you're nodding along to most of these, your immune system is asking you to look closer:

- **Year-round congestion, post-nasal drip, or sinus pressure** that never fully clears.
- **Itchy eyes, throat, or skin** that flares with no obvious pattern.
- **Bloating, gas, or loose stools** an hour or two after eating.
- **Random hives, eczema, or rashes** that come and go.
- **Headaches or brain fog** that follow specific meals.
- **Fatigue that gets worse during pollen seasons** or after takeout.

Many of these get explained away as "just allergies" or "sensitive stomach." Common: but not normal.

## What Most Doctors Miss

Standard allergy care usually means an antihistamine, a steroid spray, or a skin-prick panel limited to a handful of inhaled allergens. If the prick test comes back negative, you're told you don't have allergies.

The problem: skin-prick testing measures one type of immune response (IgE) and misses the slower, delayed reactions (IgG) that drive most food sensitivities. It also tells you nothing about *why* your immune system is reacting in the first place, whether your gut lining is leaky, whether your liver is overloaded, or whether chronic inflammation is keeping the alarm on.

A full picture asks better questions:

- **Which foods is your body producing antibodies against?**
- **Are environmental allergens stacking on top of food triggers?**
- **Is the gut barrier intact, or is it letting partial proteins through?**
- **Is the liver clearing histamine well, or is it backed up?**

When we run the right panels, we frequently find food triggers patients never suspected. And gut patterns that explain why their reactions keep multiplying.

## The 3 T's Behind Allergies

Allergies rarely happen in isolation. The Wellness Way 3 T's framework helps us look at why your immune system is on alert in the first place:

- **Toxins:** environmental chemicals, mold exposure, heavy metals, and processed-food additives all push the immune system toward over-reaction. The more loaded the system, the more it flags everyday inputs as threats.
- Traumas. Physical stress, gut injury from past antibiotic courses, or a long history of inflammation create a leaky barrier that lets undigested food particles into circulation. The body sees them as foreign and mounts a defense.
- **Thoughts** — chronic stress shifts immune balance and weakens the gut lining. Stress doesn't *cause* allergies, but it absolutely amplifies them.

You may have one of these driving the bus. You may have all three. We test to find out which.

## Our Testing Approach

We use comprehensive lab work. Not just a basic prick test. Depending on your story, your panel may include:

- **Immuno Food Allergy Test:** IgG and IgE response to ~190 common foods
