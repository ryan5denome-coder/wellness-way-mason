You don’t catch colds because you’re unlucky — you catch them when your body’s communication breaks down.

Your **nervous system** is the master control center for every function in your body, including your **immune system**. When your spine is misaligned, it can create interference in the nervous system — disrupting the brain’s ability to communicate clearly with the rest of your body. That’s where **chiropractic care** comes in.

### The Science Behind It

Research shows chiropractic adjustments can directly support immune health:

- 🧠 **Improved communication:** Adjustments help restore normal nerve flow between the brain and immune organs, allowing your body to detect and respond to threats more efficiently.

- 💪 **Increased antibodies:** A study published in _The Journal of Manipulative and Physiological Therapeutics_ found that patients showed a rise in immune antibodies (SIgA) within 30 minutes of a spinal adjustment.

- 😌 **Reduced stress hormones:** Chiropractic care has been shown to lower cortisol levels — the stress hormone that suppresses immune function — helping your body stay in balance.

- 🔄 **Less inflammation:** Adjustments can help regulate inflammatory cytokines, reducing chronic inflammation that weakens the immune system.

### The Big Picture

When your spine is aligned and your nervous system is clear, your immune system can work smarter — not harder. Regular chiropractic care keeps your body’s communication pathways open, allowing you to **adapt, heal, and defend naturally**.

If you’re ready to strengthen your immune system from the inside out, schedule an adjustment with **The Wellness Way Mason** and see how a clear spine can help you feel your best this season.
