Are you looking for a safe, natural way to support your health or your child’s development?

**CranioSacral Therapy (CST)** is a gentle, hands-on therapy that helps the body heal by improving the function of the central nervous system. It’s non-invasive, deeply relaxing, and effective for people of all ages—from newborns to elders.

## What Is CranioSacral Therapy?

CranioSacral Therapy focuses on the **craniosacral system**—the soft tissues and fluid that protect your brain and spinal cord. Stress, trauma, or illness can create tension in this system, leading to pain, discomfort, and other health challenges.

Using a **light-touch technique**, practitioners work to release these restrictions, helping the body self-correct and function at its best. CST promotes:

- Reduced pain and muscle tension

- Improved nervous system function

- Enhanced whole-body health and performance

Because it’s so gentle, CST is safe for **newborns, children, adults, and seniors**.

## What Conditions Can CST Help With?

CranioSacral Therapy has been used to support a wide range of issues, including:

- Headaches & migraines

- Chronic neck & back pain

- Stress & tension-related disorders

- Post-concussion symptoms

- Chronic fatigue & fibromyalgia

- TMJ syndrome & scoliosis

- Learning differences (ADD/ADHD)

- Chronic ear infections & hearing issues

- Digestive issues like colic & reflux in infants

- Torticollis & abnormal head shape in babies

- Immune system challenges

- Lactation and tongue/lip tie issues

…and more. Because CST helps improve nervous system function, it can benefit many areas of health naturally.

## What to Expect During a CranioSacral Session

### For Adults

Sessions take place in a **quiet, private setting**. You remain fully clothed while lying on a comfortable, padded table. The practitioner uses gentle touch to monitor fluid movement in your central nervous system and locate areas of tension or restriction.

Most sessions last **about an hour** and are deeply relaxing—many people describe a feeling of warmth, gentle pulsing, or release as the body unwinds.

### For Children and Newborns

CST is also incredibly beneficial for babies and children. Parents are encouraged to bring familiar items like toys or blankets to create comfort. The practitioner gently evaluates and releases tension patterns that may affect growth, feeding, digestion, and development.

Reactions vary—some children lie still and relax, while others may be vocal or active. This is normal, and the gentle nature of CST ensures the session remains safe and supportive.

## Why Early Intervention Matters

The birth process can place significant stress on a baby’s nervous system. Even mild restrictions can affect development and may not show up until later in childhood. **Early CST intervention** can:

- Support proper head shape and skull alignment

- Ease feeding and digestive difficulties

- Reduce the risk of developmental delays

- Strengthen the immune system naturally

Because it’s so gentle, CST integrates seamlessly with other early-intervention medical care and therapies.

## Why Is CranioSacral Therapy So Important?

Your central nervous system influences every other system in your body. When it’s functioning well, you’re healthier, stronger, and more resilient. But stress and tension can disrupt its performance.

**CST releases these restrictions**, helping your body relax, self-correct, and function optimally. Benefits may include:

- Reduced pain and stress

- Improved overall well-being

- Better sleep and emotional balance

- Stronger immune response
