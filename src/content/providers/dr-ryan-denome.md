Dr. Ryan DeNome is the clinic owner and chiropractic physician at The Wellness Way - Mason. He is passionate about helping patients overcome health challenges and reclaim their vitality. His journey into chiropractic began when his wife introduced him to the profession, and he immediately recognized the perfect blend of hands-on problem-solving and patient care.

He pursued his Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Life University in Marietta, Georgia, where he discovered The Wellness Way Student Club during his first week. For nearly four years, he immersed himself in The Wellness Way philosophy — preparing to serve patients beyond conventional chiropractic care.

Dr. Ryan is especially passionate about supporting men who feel limited by health challenges, and moms juggling the stress of home life and wellness struggles. His goal is to offer hope to those who feel stuck — providing comprehensive care that addresses the underlying drivers of symptoms instead of just managing them.

Certified in the Webster Technique, he is also dedicated to caring for pregnant mothers, everyday athletes, blue-collar workers, gym-goers, and hobbyists who want to optimize their health and performance.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Ryan enjoys spending time outdoors with his wife and son — hiking, kayaking, or simply soaking in the beauty of nature. A lifelong adventurer, he and his wife biked 100 miles on their second date.
