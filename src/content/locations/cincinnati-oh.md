If you've been told *"your labs are fine"* by a UC Health internist or a Christ Hospital specialist and your body is telling you otherwise — your body is right to keep asking.

## Our Approach: Test, Don't Guess

We use the Wellness Way framework: the 3 T's (Traumas, Toxins, Thoughts) as a map for what's driving inflammation in your case, and a set of named lab panels (DUTCH, Cardiometabolic, TWW Basic, Immuno Food Allergy, No Sugar Panel) to actually measure what's happening underneath. We build a Health Restoration Plan around your specific results, not a generic protocol.

We do not replace your medical team. We complement it. If you've got a UC Health cardiologist managing blood pressure or a Christ Hospital endocrinologist following thyroid antibodies, we work alongside that care, not in place of it.
